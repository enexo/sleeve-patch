Welcome to SleevePatches.com

Sleeve Patches, otherwise known as Shoulder Sleeve Insignias (SSI) are worn by United States Army members and other militia units across the globe. In the US Army, SSI are worn on the upper left sleeve of the uniform. An additional SSI may be worn on the upper right sleeve to denote former wartime service. Generally speaking, SSI come in two versions - camouflaged or full color. Camouflaged or subdued SSI are worn on combat uniforms while full color SSI are reserved for Class A and Mess Dress uniforms. A full description of SSI can be found on Wikipedia

Here on SleevePatches, we have broken our catalog of SSI into multiple groups based on usage. Each picture of SSI comes with a link to the Wikipedia page of the military unit.
